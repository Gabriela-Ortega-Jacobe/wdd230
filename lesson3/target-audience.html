Pilar City Chamber of Commerce

By Gabriela Ortega

Home Page Audience Design

Target Audience

They are the owners of small and medium businesses. That they are looking for a way to grow their business, find the best suppliers, and have the benefits that the chamber of commerce can offer them. They seek to grow and work to employ the citizens of Pilar.

Personas

Teresa


Teresa is a 45-year-old, single mother of two, who after the pandemic started to take her hobby to the next level. She is currently working full-time with her Agenda business. She is enthusiastic and active that never stops learning and she is willing to push her small business to an all new level.

Luis


Luis is the owner of a medium-sized company with 10 employees. He is a man who has been working non-stop since he was 15 years old, and now he is 64 years old. He founded a small mattress factory and with the economic instability he went from being a great businessman to reducing his production by half, he continues to have a good prestige in the community.

Scenarios

  • They enter to look for other entrepreneurs in the field.
  • They look to improve their audience so they look to their competitors.
  • They enter to Share best practices
  • They enter to search for available training.
  • They enter to find information about benefits that the chamber gives according to the status of their company or business./li>
